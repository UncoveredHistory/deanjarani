About Us

KF Tirana is the most successful football club in Albania with a rich history spanning over a century. With 25 league titles and countless achievements in European competitions, our club represents the pride and passion of Albanian football. We are committed to excellence, developing young talent, and bringing glory to Tirana and Albania.
